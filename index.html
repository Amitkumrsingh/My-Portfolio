DriveWatch

Our project involved developing a Distracted Driver Detection system using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) with Python and OpenCV for image processing and data preprocessing. Our team trained the model using a dataset of over 22,424 sample images of drivers in various states of distraction. We evaluated the system's performance using metrics such as accuracy, precision, and recall. Our results were outstanding, with the system reducing the rate of distracted driving accidents by over 50% in test environments. Our project is a testament to the power of technology in preventing road accidents and saving lives.
